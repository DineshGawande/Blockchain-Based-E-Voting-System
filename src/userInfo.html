Welcome

Guidelines for Users

1. Voter Registration

  • Users must register before voting.
  • Registration is only allowed during the registration phase.
  • Users must provide their Aadhaar number and account address.
  • Age verification is required (must be 18+).
  • OTP verification is necessary for successful registration.

2. Voting Process

  1. Registration Phase: Users can register during this phase.
  2. Voting Phase: Users can vote by clicking on the "VOTE" button.
  3. Result Phase: Results are displayed in the "Results" section.
